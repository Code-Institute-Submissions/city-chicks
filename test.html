You will need to make sure you have enough space to house your chickens in your backyard Two square meters are required for every 4 hens you keep. Build or buy a coop from here xxxxxlinkxxxxx. Have water and food dispensers ready.

There are two popular options here, you could either buy brand new hens from a breeder, or if you want to be more adventurous, you can adopt hens after they have been rescued from battery farms. Below are links to where you can get either of these and also some pros and cons you should consider before making your choice.

Before you collect your hens, you will want to stock up on some feed, bedding, accessories and medicine. We have added some great links to the resources page for you to get acess to all the things you need.

Once you have your hen home set up, all you need to do is collect them and bring them home. Your hens will take a few days to settle in to their new environment, it is recommended that you keep an eye on them and visit them as often as you are able to, to make sure they are getting on alright.
